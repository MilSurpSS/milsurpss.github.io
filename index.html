Military Surplus Support and Solutions

MilSurpSS is based out of Austin, TX, USA. We are a fledgling movement to create a self supporting organization for the benefit of all communities. We hope to supply assistance to not only those in times of need but also the gracious volunteers and support personnel responding and tending to those in need.

Services

Here's a list of things we can do for you. While we can cook and clean, creating systems to nuture in the long run are preferable. The funds we raise go to support operational expenses and procurment of supplies and equipment.

    Rainwater catchment systems
    Raised bed gardens
    Fencing
